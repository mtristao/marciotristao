WORK

In 2001, I joined Globo.com as an Information Architect (IA). A few years later I became the IA team manager. During this period, I contributed to important websites such as Globo.com (portal), G1, GloboEsporte.com, Ego...

Another important point was the "Standardization Project", on which I was one of the leads. This project had the objective of creating a fundamental group of principles and rules to design Globo.com's identity, including a proprietary UX style. As a result of this work all products were redesigned to bring to the whole portal a uniform user experience and other benefits.

Simultaneously with the IA team management I assumed, for some time, the function of Product Owner (PO) of the portal. This work gave me an important view about business. Few years after I assumed as the manager for news-based products at Globo.com.

Today I am responsible for the development of Sports Products. I was pleased to lead the teams responsible for the projects of the Football World Cup in 2014 and the Olympic Games in 2016. I also actively participated in the redesign of an important Brazilian Fantasy Game (Cartola).

At the beginning of my career I also worked on the creation of the websites of BR Distribuidora and Petrobras (holding). In parallel, I contributed to specialized magazines and sites like Internet Business, Carta.com, Carta Capital and Web Insider with articles on business and technology.
